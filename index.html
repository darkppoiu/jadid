Questions

Frequently Asked

Is this free to join? +
Yes, completely free. There are no fees, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges. You simply sign up, choose a task, complete it, and earn your reward.
How long do tasks take? +
Most tasks take between 3 and 6 minutes to complete. The estimated time is shown on each task card so you know exactly what to expect before you start.
How many tasks can I complete per day? +
New tasks are added daily. The available slots are limited to ensure quality, so we recommend checking back each day for fresh opportunities.
Do I need special skills or equipment? +
No special skills required. All you need is a device with internet access. Tasks are designed to be simple and straightforward for anyone to complete.
